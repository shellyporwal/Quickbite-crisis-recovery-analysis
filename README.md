
#  QuickBite Crisis Recovery Analysis

## Project Overview

QuickBite Express, a leading food delivery startup, experienced a major operational disruption in mid-2025. During this period, the platform witnessed a significant decline in customer activity, increased delivery delays, and falling customer satisfaction ratings.

This project analyzes pre-crisis and crisis-period performance to identify the root causes of the decline, evaluate customer and restaurant behavior, and recommend data-driven strategies to support business recovery.

---

##  Business Objective

The goal of this analysis was to:

* Assess the impact of the crisis on business performance
* Identify operational and customer-related pain points
* Analyze customer retention and churn behavior
* Evaluate restaurant and city-level performance
* Develop actionable recovery strategies based on insights

---

* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboards and business analytics

## Key Insights

### Business Performance

* Total orders declined by **69%**, indicating a sharp contraction in customer demand.
* Net revenue decreased by **70.9%** during the crisis period.
* Average Order Value (AOV) declined by **6.4%**, suggesting demand reduction rather than spending behavior changes.

### Operational Performance

* Cancellation rate increased by **5.9%**.
* SLA compliance dropped by **31.4%**.
* Average delivery time increased from **39.5 minutes to 60.1 minutes** (**+52.1%**), highlighting severe operational inefficiencies.

### Customer Experience

* Average customer ratings fell from **4.5 to 2.5**.
* **84.48%** of loyal customers churned during the crisis.
* **76.76%** of reviews were negative, primarily driven by:

  * Delivery delays
  * Food quality concerns
  * Food safety issues

### Restaurant Performance

* Active restaurants declined by **16.7%**, reducing platform supply.
* Restaurants such as **Royal Curry Mahal** and **Taste of Cafe Corner** experienced the steepest decline in orders.

### City-Level Insights

* **Bangalore** generated the highest revenue overall.
* Despite its strong contribution, Bangalore lost **71.4%** of its loyal customer base.
* Operational issues were consistent across cities, indicating a platform-wide problem rather than location-specific challenges.

### Customer Acquisition

* Organic channels contributed **51.75%** of total customer acquisitions.
* Strong organic demand suggests significant recovery potential through retention-focused initiatives.

---

##  Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Customer Retention

* Launch targeted win-back campaigns for churned loyal customers.
* Implement loyalty rewards and personalized offers for high-value users.

### 2. Delivery Optimization

* Improve delivery partner allocation and route optimization.
* Reduce delivery delays through better operational monitoring.

### 3. Service Quality Improvement

* Strengthen food safety and packaging standards.
* Increase restaurant performance monitoring and quality audits.

### 4. Restaurant Support

* Identify underperforming restaurant partners.
* Provide operational support and performance improvement programs.

### 5. Customer Experience Enhancement

* Improve ETA accuracy and delivery transparency.
* Reduce cancellations through proactive communication and issue resolution.

### 6. Focus on High-Value Markets

* Prioritize retention efforts in Bangalore and other high-revenue markets.
* Design targeted campaigns for loyal and high-spending customer segments.

### 7. Growth Strategy

* Leverage strong organic acquisition through referral programs.
* Introduce customer loyalty initiatives to improve retention and lifetime value.
